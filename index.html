Welcome to Catalyst Tutoring

We are a small workers' cooperative of year 12 high school students offering high-quality, affordable private tutoring to help you succeed.

About Us

We are a passionate group of year 12 students from Perth Modern School who excel in various subjects and have a desire to help others succeed academically.

We believe in a curiosity-focused approach, using real-world examples and conversational learning to build our students' motivation and confidence. We offer in-person tutoring for students between years 5 and 10, in Mathematics, Science, and English.

Logistics

Our usual rate is $35 per hour. Sessions are in person, usually on Saturdays, between 10:00 AM and 5:00 PM at our office in East Perth, but timing and locations may be flexible.
